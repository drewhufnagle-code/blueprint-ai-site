Values

Principles That Save You Time.

Process First, Tech Second

Technology amplifies your process. If we automate a bad process, we just create chaos faster. We fix the workflow before we add the AI.

Clarity Over Complexity

If a system is too hard for your team to use, it has failed. We design for the human on the other side of the screen, keeping things simple.

Empowerment, Not Dependency

We don't build "black boxes" that only we can fix. We document everything and train you to run the system without us.

About

Hi, I’m Drew.

I’m the founder of Blueprint AI Consulting Co. I help business owners and everyday people simplify life and work with practical AI, automation, and simple systems that reduce stress and give you real time back.


My path into this started with a Bachelor of Science from the University of Pittsburgh, then work at Uber Advanced Technologies Group, where I was around early machine learning and automation. I saw what AI can do when it is built correctly. I also saw why most people bounce off it. It feels technical, unclear, and disconnected from real routines.


I’m also a high school baseball coach. Coaching shaped how I do this work. You do not improve by hearing a speech or buying a tool. You improve with fundamentals, a plan, repetition, and someone who can teach it in a way that fits your day. That is my approach with AI.


AI is here whether we like it or not. The people who learn how to use it faster, in a practical way, will be in a better position. My job is to make that learning curve easier.


If you want less chaos and a setup that actually works day to day, Blueprint AI is built for you.
